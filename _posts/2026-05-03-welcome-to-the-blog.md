This is a sample post. Replace it or delete it when you add your own content.

To write a new post, add a file under `_posts/` named with today’s date and a short slug, for example:

`2026-05-15-my-topic.md`

At the top of the file, use YAML front matter:

```yaml
---
title: "Your post title"
---
```

Optional fields you can add include `subtitle`, or a custom `excerpt` if you do not want Jekyll to infer one from the first paragraph.

Posts are listed on the **Blog** page (`/blog/`) and included in the Atom feed at `/feed.xml` via **jekyll-feed**.
